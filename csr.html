Our CSR strategy

The CSR program is carried out by our operating companies in the community that we serve. We believe our success has been made possible by the support of the community, and by giving back to and empowering the surrounding communities, we help creating a positive cycle of growth. MUTU's CSR program addresses the following aspects: health, education, community development and environment. These four aspects mutually support and strengthen one another for sustainable long term gains. In order to realize gains in these four areas, MUTU focuses on three areas: Community Relations and Development, Infrastructure Development, and Nurturing the Environment.
