Product Specification

Through our operating companies, IIR produces high calorific steam coal, which is mostly used for steam generation as well as for fuel supplements by various industries. Our coal specification offers customers the advantage of high heat value with low ash content for efficient coal usage.

The coal specification is analyzed by independent third parties to ensure that the quality consistently meets our customers’ requirements.
